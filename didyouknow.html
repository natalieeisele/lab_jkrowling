Did you know?

J.K. Rowling has won many awards and donated a lot of money to different philanthropies. These include:

  • Awarded Order of the British Empire (OBE) by Prince Charles (2001)
  • Donated $17 million to Comic Releif charity from book profits (2001)
  • Unveiling of her portrait in National Portait Gallery of London (2005)
  • Delivers commemcement address at Harvard University (2008)
  • Receives honorary degree from Harvard University (2008)
  • Donated $1 million to the Labour Party (2008)
  • Awarded Legion of Honor by French President Nicolas Sarkozy (2009)
  • Donated $10 million to research clinic at the University of Edinburgh in honor of her mother (2010)
  • Granted Freedom of the City of London (2012)
  • Book of her Harvard University commencement address is released (2015)
