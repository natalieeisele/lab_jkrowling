Biography

J.K. Rowling is a well-known author worldwide. Born in Yate, England in 1965, Rowling then went on to graduate from Exeter University. After living in Portugal with her first husband, Rowling moved to Scotland with their daughter after divorcing. In 1999, Rowling published the first book in the Harry Potter series. She later published six more in the series and was on the New York Times bestseller list. Her books were then adopted into movies. Rowling also published The Casual Vacancy, and other works under the pseudonym Robert Galbraith.
