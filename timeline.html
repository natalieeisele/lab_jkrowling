Timeline

  • 1965: Born
  • 1971: Wrote first book, "Rabbit"
  • 1990: Idea for Harry Potter while traveling by train
  • 1991-1993: Taught English in Portugal
  • 1994: Began writing Harry Potter in coffee shops
  • 1997: "Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone" (first book) published in U.K.
  • 1998: "Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone" published in U.S. with new title
  • 1998: "Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets published in U.S., published in U.K. previous year
  • 1999: "Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban" published
  • 2000: "Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire" published
  • 2001: "Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them" and "Quidditch Through the Ages" published as add-on books to the Harry Potter series
  • 2001: First film released
  • 2003: “Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix” published
  • 2005:“Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince" published
  • 2007: “Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows" published
  • 2010: The Wizarding World of Harry Potter opens at Universal Studios in Orlando
  • 2011: Announces Pottermore.com through YouTube announcement
  • 2012: Pottermore goes live
  • 2012: "The Casual Vacancy" published, first book geared toward adults
  • 2012: Reveals pseudonym Robert Galbraith

Source: CNN
